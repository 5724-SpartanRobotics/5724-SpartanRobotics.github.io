Spartan Robotics

FRC Team #5724 based at Salem High School in Salem, Virginia.

We are a 5th year robotics team (in 2019) that competes each year in the FIRST Robotics Competition. Starting in January, we are tasked with designing, building, and programming a robot to solve a unique challenge and compete with schools in a worldwide competition organized by FIRST (For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology).
