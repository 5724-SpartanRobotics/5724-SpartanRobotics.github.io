Blue Ridge Brawl

What is Blue Ridge Brawl?
Blue Ridge Brawl is an Off-season FIRST Robotics Competition event hosted by FRC team 5724 Spartan Robotics. We will be playing FIRST Destination Deep Space in October 2019.

When: Friday, October 4, and Saturday, October 5, 2019.
Where: Salem High School, 400 Spartan Drive, Salem, VA 24153
Fee: $300 Per Robot
Registration: Contact blueridgebrawl@gmail.com for a registration package and details. Forms will be available here soon.

The event will be free and open to the public (registration fee for teams).
