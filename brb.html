What is the Blue Ridge Brawl?

A number of high school robotics teams will come together to play FIRST Destination Deep Space. Our goal is to provide an event where new teams, new drivers, and new members to the FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) community are welcome and can engage in friendly competition.
