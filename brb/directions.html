Directions - Blue Ridge Brawl

Location and Directions

The Blue Ridge Brawl will take place at:
Salem High School
400 Spartan Drive
Salem, VA 24153

From I-81 North or South, take exit 137 towards Salem. Keep right at the fork and follow signs for Salem / Civic Center. Merge onto Wildwood Road. In about half a mile, to turn left onto West Main Street. After about a quarter of a mile, turn left onto Spartan Drive to enter through the front gates.

Parking and Event Load-In

Optional early team load-in will begin at 5:00 PM Friday. Regular team load-in will begin Saturday at 7:15 AM. Below is the load-in map. Trailers and buses can be parked along Tennessee Street on the school/track side of the road. General parking (for both teams and spectators) is in the Salem School Administrative Offices lot across 4th street from the school. Teams will load-in in the drop-off area at the front of the school along S College Avenue.
