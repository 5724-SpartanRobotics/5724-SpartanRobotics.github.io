--- title: Blue Ridge Brawl Offseason FRC Event date: Friday, October 4, 2019 end_date: Saturday, October 5, 2019 --- The Blue Ridge Brawl is an offseason FRC event hosted by Spartan Robotics (FRC 5724). It is located at Salem High School, 400 Spartan Drive, Salem, VA 24153 and is free and open to the public for spectators (on Saturday). The registration fee for teams to compete is $300.

Load-in will be Friday evening and the competition will take place Saturday. Please see https://shsrobotics.us/brb for more information.
The event's The Blue Alliance page can be found here: https://www.thebluealliance.com/event/2019vabrb
