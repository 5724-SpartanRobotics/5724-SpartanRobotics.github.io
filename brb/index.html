Blue Ridge Brawl

What is Blue Ridge Brawl?
Blue Ridge Brawl is an off-season FIRST Robotics Competition event hosted by FRC team 5724 Spartan Robotics. We will be playing FIRST CRESCENDO in October 2024!

When: Saturday, October 19, 2024 (with optional early load-in Friday, October 18)
Where: {% comment %} IMPORTANT NOTE: make sure that if you change this address, you also change the Google Maps link! {% endcomment %} Andrew Lewis Middle School, 616 S College Ave, Salem, VA 24153
Please see directions and parking for directions to the venue and load-in and parking instructions.
Fee: $350 Per Team (half price for each additional robot and for teams whose rookie year was 2024)
