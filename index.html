Spartan Robotics

FRC Team #5724 based at Salem High School in Salem, Virginia.

We are a 5th year robotics team that competes each year in the FIRST Robotics Competition. Starting in January, we are tasked with designing, building, and programming a robot to solve a unique challenge and compete with schools in a worldwide competition organized by FIRST (For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology). For upcoming events see the Team Calendar.
If you want to purchase team gear, including shirts, hats, and polos, these can be bought from the Fuzzy Pickle online at http://fuzzypickletshirts.com/spartanrobotics/shop/home.
